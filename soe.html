Marine & Offshore Technology

Fascinated by ships and how they work? Set sail on your maritime journey when you join our Diploma in Marine & Offshore Technology (MOT). We will train you in naval architecture and offshore technology, which are among the most sought-after specialist skills in Singapore’s maritime industry. With MOT, you will learn to design and build your own ship models, and test them in Singapore’s only towing tank located in our campus. Our strong emphasis on integrated real-world projects will give you an edge in creating innovative solutions for using clean energy, developing new materials and processes, as well as designing and building marine vessels and offshore structures.
