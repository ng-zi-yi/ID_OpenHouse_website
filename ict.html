Common ICT Programme

Interested in the world of infocomm technology but unsure about which course to choose? With the Common ICT Programme (CICTP), you will have more time to explore different disciplines before making a more informed choice. During the first semester, you will get an introduction to the field of ICT and gain an understanding of the roles, practices and career paths of ICT professionals. You will also learn the fundamentals of programming, design principles, cybersecurity concepts, as well as data science.
